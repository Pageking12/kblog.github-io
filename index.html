ABOUT

why  do we people travel to a place? 

People travel for diverse reasons, seeking adventure, exploration, and the thrill of discovering new cultures. Travel offers relaxation, an escape from routine, and a chance to unwind in different landscapes. Education and learning drive some to explore historical sites and diverse traditions. Business needs often compel travel for meetings and conferences. Human connections motivate journeys to reunite with family and friends, fostering meaningful relationships across distances. Ultimately, travel fulfills a myriad of desires—be it curiosity, relaxation, education, or interpersonal bonds—creating experiences that enrich lives and broaden perspectives.
